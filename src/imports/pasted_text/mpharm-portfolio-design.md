Design Goal: Create a clean, high-end, and professional personal portfolio website for a Master of Pharmacy student. The design should immediately convey trust, precision, innovation, and expertise in the pharmaceutical field.

Target Audience: Recruiters, hiring managers, and pharmaceutical industry professionals.

Key Themes: Professional, Modern, Eye-Catching, Scientific, Precision, Growth, Health.

Color Palette:

Primary: Fresh Pharmaceutical Green (e.g., #2ECC71 or #27AE60) - Represents health, safety, growth, and organic compounds.

Secondary: Professional Medical Blue (e.g., #3498DB or #2980B9) - Represents trust, stability, technology, and corporate professionalism.

Accent: Light sky blue (for gradients/UI highlights) and pure white (for clean background).

Text: Dark charcoal or navy blue for readability (avoid pure black).

Typography:

Headings: Modern, strong, sans-serif font (e.g., Poppins, Montserrat, or Inter) to give it an authoritative tech/scientific feel.

Body: Clean, highly legible serif or sans-serif (e.g., Lato, Open Sans, or Roboto).

Overall Design Principles for Figma:
Whitespace: Generous use of white space (negative space) between elements to keep it airy and professional.

Consistency: Ensure all buttons, headings, and card sizes are consistent (using Figma's Auto Layout feature).

Micro-Interactions: Add subtle hover effects (e.g., button grows slightly darker, card casts a slight shadow on hover) for a modern feel.

Mobile Responsiveness: The design should be adaptable for a desktop (1440px width) and mobile view (360px width).
